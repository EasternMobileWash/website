About Us

Eastern Mobile Wash is a mobile power cleaning service equipped for a wide variety of jobs. Operating 4 seasons and 7 days a week, located in Cornwall Ontario and serving the eastern side of the province, we're here to tackle any job big or small.

We provide onsite professional hot and cold water cleaning services, with guaranteed satisfaction.

Starting out by serving the local community. Now serving the entire eastern side of the province.

Eastern Mobile Wash was started in Williamstown ON at the beginning of the COVID 19 pandemic, by 2 brothers, Owen and Oliver Hughes.

Frequently Asked Questions

Questions? Everything you need to know about Eastern Mobile Wash is just a phone call away, but here's som e quick bits about us.

  • Do you Operate Year round?

    Yes we operate 7 days a week 365 days a year. We set up our unit to withstand the warmest and coldest of temperatures.

  • What products do you use?

    We work with a soap manufacturer that does custom blend soaps, acids and surfactants. Overall we stick to 4 solutions, Non scrub soap, aluminum brightener (acid), heavy degreaser, and a non tarnishing acid (stainless steel) we have this range of products that are able to tackle whatever comes our way.

  • What is your travel charge outside of your area?

    We serve the entire eastern side of Ontario, regardless of where you are located we come to you!

  • How do you properly clean the peak of my building, structure, house etc?

    We use wands that have a reach of up to 24 feet, most buildings we can get to those tough spots, if not we use ladders or a genie boom to make sure we can get to those tough to reach spots.
